We begin this session near [[Duskwatch]]. It is a little nepali village atop a spire. There are llamapaca. We ask around to the vendors to see if they sell berries.They have been having trouble reaching the top of the summit to procure berries. The matriarch mountain crab is overly hostile and has prevented them from collecting more berries. People are angry that they can't make wine without them.

We try to purchase a grappling gun but we remember that we are poor as shit. We sell the 2 poison saps to him for 5 handfuls of gold.

We try to talk to a sherpa and head to the Sherpa Shack and see a bunch there. We talk to them about how hard it is to come by berries now adays due to the matriarch. Apparently this started a few weeks ago. They think she is just hungry and they like eating llamapacas and harpies.

We agree to pay a faun to help us get to the top. His name is [[Stanley]]. If we do well, he'll split the profits with us. We decide to go back and purchase the grappling hook.

We start up the mountain. We make it pretty high up before [[Slate]] shoots a grappling hook at a mountain crab. We then are attacked by it. [[Sheldon]] flies up and uses Blunderbussy to shoot the attacking crab and blows them off the top of the spire. The rest of the party attacks some other mountain crabs that appear. We defeat all three crabs.

We continue to the top of the spire and are attacked by the matriarch. We are swarmed by a bunch of other crabs and kill the matriarch almost immediately. We start cleaning up the remaining crabs. We continue fighting the crabs over and over again! We kill everything and collect all of the berry bushels and half of the clutch of eggs. We also harvest the carapace of the matriarch and get some trophies.

We head back to town and sell all the bushels and get our 4 bags of gold. We also sell the clutch of eggs for 5 bags of gold. We all leveled up.

We head back to [[Hush]].
