We begin our session in [[Root's Hollow]]. We need to decide between helping the narcos vs the big pharma. We are just outside the place where the meeting took place.

[[Slate]] sneaks up and overhears the elders say that maybe [[Hellbender]] must not be happy with the latest batch of sap.

The party confronts the elders. [[Sheldon]] says that they need purified sap or else it creates earthquakes and causes damage. [[Durok]] and [[Lysa]] get named as the narcos to the elders to try and get us more cred. We learn that [[Durok]] is dumping bad sap into the earth after each batch. After much deliberation, we decide that we will steal the recipe for the sap from [[Meylis]]. We couldn't come up with a good idea so we just started attacking everyone. We fight everyone and begin robbing the store. We knock everyone out and tie them up.

We find a chest but aren't able to pick it, so [[Slate]] tries to smash it open with his pickaxe and is successful.

We find 2 recipes, minor stamina and minor health. We can use a bone of a creature to make the stamina potion or a vial of blood for the potion of health. 

We are able to get the recipe out of [[Meylis]]. "it is made with love". We also take the rest of her sap inventory. We hand out the sap that we stole to the neighborhood and heal up many of the people. We go and speak with [[Durok]] and [[Lysa]] and convince [[Lysa]] to collaborate with [[Meylis]]. We leave the town and rest.

We decide to head to [[Titan's Steps]] to find another ingredient and cut through the forest to save time instead of going back through [[Hush]]. We are looking for bugbane berry.

We arrive and there are a ton of rope bridges that are scraggly with cargo safety nets beneath.

There is an information panel that says there are a ton of rope bridges and spires. It warns of mountain crabs which can be small or the size of a house. And the bugbane berries grow on the tallest spire.
